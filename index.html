About Us

At Cozy Crumbs Bakery, we've been your neighborhood bakery since 1990. Our passion for artisanal baking started with a family recipe and grew into a community favorite.

We bake everything fresh daily, using locally sourced ingredients and traditional methods passed down through generations. From our rustic sourdoughs to sweet pastries, every bite tells a story of love and craftsmanship.

Our mission is to create a warm, welcoming space where families and friends gather, celebrate, and enjoy delicious baked goods made with heart.
