Our Story

For over three decades, Cozy Crumbs Bakery has been the heart of our community, filling homes and hearts with the irresistible aroma of freshly baked goods. What started as a small family dream in 1990 has blossomed into a beloved local institution.

Every morning before dawn, our passionate bakers begin their craft, mixing, kneading, and shaping dough with the same dedication and love that our founders instilled from day one.
