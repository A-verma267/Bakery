Our Legacy of Love & Loaves

Three generations of baking excellence

Our Journey Through Time

From humble beginnings to community cornerstone, discover the rich history that makes Cozy Crumbs special.

1990 - The Dream Begins

Margaret and Thomas Crumb open their first small bakery with just two ovens and a dream to serve the community with authentic, homemade breads and pastries.

1995 - First Award

Our signature sourdough wins "Best Artisan Bread" at the Regional Bakery Competition, putting Cozy Crumbs on the culinary map.

2005 - Next Generation

The Crumb children, Sarah and Michael, join the family business, bringing fresh ideas while honoring traditional recipes.

2015 - Grand Expansion

We expand to our current location, doubling our space and adding a cozy café area where customers can enjoy fresh baked goods with coffee.

Today - Legacy Continues

35 years strong, we continue to serve our community with the same passion, now with the third generation learning the craft.

Our Baking Philosophy

Freshness First

We bake fresh every day, using only the finest ingredients sourced from local farms and trusted suppliers. No preservatives, no shortcuts—just pure, wholesome goodness.

Time-Honored Traditions

Our recipes have been passed down through generations, each one perfected over decades. We believe in respecting the craft while embracing innovation.

Community Connection

We're more than a bakery—we're part of the community fabric. From supporting local events to providing employment, we're committed to giving back.
